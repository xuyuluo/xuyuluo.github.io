Overview of attention mechanism

General speaking, the output of all the operations we have used such as fully connected layers, convolutions or poolings is mainly driven by its local input value. The arising of attention mechanism brings two main changes:

  1. depending on the feature themselves, not on their positions,
  2. relaxing locality constraints.

These two changes are ascribed to the dynamic modulation of the weighting of different parts of a signal which permits the presentation and allocation of information channels to be dependent on the activations themselves.
